When a Loved One Has Dementia 
###########################################

About this Guide 
================

.. image:: images/Grands.jpg
   :width: 280pt
   
Caretaking of a parent does not come naturally to most of us.  There is an inevitable reversal of roles when a parent or other beloved elder begins to demonstrate cognitive decline. For most, it requires a giant leap toward accepting the reality of this new relationship dynamic. This guide is designed to help family members understand the early stages of dementia and the responsibilities that come with this new role.
